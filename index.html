About Us

DeFitax is a defi approach for calculating crypto tax.

According to our findings, Crypto tax is still the most challenging, particularly for decentralized transactions. We are the first DeFi tax computation solution, and we supply the solution by employing the NEAR protocol's sophisticated technology.
